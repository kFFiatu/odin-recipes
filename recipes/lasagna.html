Lasagna

Om nom nom

This is the most delicious lasagna you'll ever taste. The best part is that you can make it in your home and share with your family and friends! Let's get started!

Ingredients :

  • Meat - beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsley and basil
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
