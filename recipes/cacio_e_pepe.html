Cacio e pepe

Great, simple pasta, trust me!

So simple but so delicious. You should definitely try this easy-to-prepare pasta!

Ingredients :

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1Â¾ cups grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring pot of lightly salted water to boil
  2. Cook spaghetti
  3. Reserve 1 cup cooking water
  4. Drain spaghetti
  5. Mix water and cheese and put on top of pasta
  6. Add garlic and mix everything together
